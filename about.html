The OrenkaFine House

Our philosophy

The world is my catwalk

OrenkaFine was founded on a simple idea: fine jewelry should be worn, not saved for occasions. Our founder began sketching pieces at café tables across Europe, gathering color and form from wherever life took her.

Today the house designs two distinct lines from the same studio — OrenkaFine Jewelry, our fine 18kt gold collection, and OrenkaFine Moda, our fashion-forward everyday pieces — united by the same hand-finished craftsmanship.

Shop the collections

Craftsmanship

Made to be layered, mixed, and lived in

Every setting is finished by hand in small batches. We work in 18kt gold, sterling silver, and considered gemstones — amethyst, emerald, London blue topaz — chosen for how they catch the light against skin.

We design in families, not single pieces, so a ring, a stack of bangles and a pair of earrings from the same collection always feel like they belong together.

Our journey

Three decades of craft

1994

The first sketch

Our founder sketches her first ring on a café napkin in Amsterdam — the piece that would become the Amsterdam collection.

2006

Milano atelier opens

A second studio opens in Milano, bringing Italian goldsmithing traditions into the OrenkaFine design language.

2015

OrenkaFine Moda launches

A fashion-forward sister line launches, making everyday layering pieces accessible alongside our fine jewelry.

2026

Worn worldwide

OrenkaFine pieces are now sold through boutiques and retail partners across three continents.
