Our Story

Since 1996

Business Begins With Trust

Before a design is selected, before a diamond is set, and before a piece reaches the display counter, there is one question that matters most: can the retailer trust the people behind the jewellery?

OrenkaFine was built to answer that question with confidence. Founded in Mumbai in 1996, the brand began with a deep respect for the discipline of the gems and jewellery trade and a clear purpose: to manufacture fine jewellery that retailers could sell with pride and certainty.

Every piece had to carry more than design. It had to carry accuracy, finish, durability, documentation, and the assurance that the work behind it was honest and dependable.

Our craft

A Story Built on Craftsmanship

The journey of OrenkaFine has always been connected to craftsmanship. Jewellery is a refined art, but it is also a serious responsibility. A small difference in stone selection, setting, polish, proportion, or finish can change the value and beauty of a piece.

For nearly three decades, the company has grown by respecting detail, learning from the trade, listening to retailers, and treating every order as a commitment.

The company continues to bring together skilled craftsmanship, careful processes, and a deep understanding of jewellery excellence. Trust remains central. Quality remains essential. Integrity remains the difference between a transaction and a lasting relationship.

Growth

From Craft to Company

OrenkaFine began as a manufacturing business with a clear purpose: to create fine jewellery that retailers could sell with confidence.

Over the years, the company built its strength quietly and steadily. Retailers returned because quality was consistent, communication was clear, delivery commitments were respected, and the founders remained accessible.

Throughout this journey, every stage of growth was guided by the same philosophy: long-term relationships are built through reliability, transparency, and attention to detail. Rather than pursuing rapid expansion, the company focused on strengthening its capabilities, refining its craftsmanship, and creating a foundation that retailers could continue to trust.

As the business evolved, OrenkaFine strengthened its systems, expanded its manufacturing discipline, and invested in better processes without losing the personal accountability that shaped the company from the beginning.

Rooted in Mumbai

The Mumbai Foundation

Mumbai has always been at the heart of OrenkaFine. As one of India's leading centres for diamonds, jewellery, manufacturing, and trade, the city provides the ideal environment where craftsmanship, expertise, and commerce come together.

OrenkaFine operates from its trade office at Bharat Diamond Bourse, BKC, and its manufacturing facility in Andheri East. This strategic presence enables the company to maintain close coordination between production and trade while ensuring every piece is crafted under direct supervision.

With complete oversight of the manufacturing process and a strong connection to the jewellery trade, the company delivers consistent quality, timely execution, and the flexibility to meet the evolving requirements of retailers and business partners.

This integrated Mumbai-based foundation reflects OrenkaFine's commitment to precision, reliability, and lasting business relationships, combining skilled craftsmanship with efficient operations to serve customers with confidence.

The people behind the name

The Craft Behind the Name

At OrenkaFine, the craft begins with understanding the piece: its purpose, design language, material, stones, karat, finish, and the customer it is meant for.

The company works in 18K, 14K, and 9K gold, with designs made in yellow gold, white gold, and rose gold finishes. Each piece is handled with attention to proportion, setting security, wearability, polish, and commercial suitability. Our process is shaped by disciplined development: understanding the requirement, analysing practicality, refining the design, preparing materials, executing with skill, and checking the finished piece before delivery.

Looking ahead

The Next Chapter

The goal ahead is to grow stronger: with better systems, wider reach, deeper retailer relationships, and continued commitment to certified, consistent, in-house jewellery manufacturing.

OrenkaFine is a brand built for retailers who value reliability as much as design, documentation as much as beauty, and partnership as much as product. The story began in 1996. It continues today with every piece we make, every order we deliver, and every relationship we build.
